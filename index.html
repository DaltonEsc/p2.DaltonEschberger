C Grade Section

  • Dashboard Example 1

    Pros

    • Very simplistic design
    • Low amount of bloat or menu, lowers driver distraction
    • Speed is the most centered and largest section
    • Left side gauges are easy to read
    • Light up indicators are easy to read

    Cons

    • Three zones are very similar
    • Left side gear select is not very large
    • Small digital text is hard to read
    • No other options for indicators/gauges
    • No customization

  • Dashboard Example 2

    Pros

    • Large tachometer, since manual it is important
    • Oil temp gauge in the top left
    • Simplistic design

    Cons

    • Sunken in gauges mark is hard to read in some angles
    • Monochromatic color scheme makes it hard to differentiate between components
    • Small digital screen is haard to read
    • Very emphasized for "sport" car style

  • Dashboard Example 3

    Pros

    • Very simplistic
    • Shows all basic/needed information without paging
    • Looks to allow for some customization of middle screen
    • Both speed/rpm are very easily readable

    Cons

    • Positioning draws eyes to middle screen, away from the speed/rpm
    • Low color differentiation
    • Text size seems small for quick glances

  • Dashboard Example 4

    Pros

    • Integrated maps to allow for easy viewing
    • Large text and gauges
    • All information viewable on one screen
    • iphone integration for calls/phone information

    Cons

    • May be distracting due to a lot of information at once
    • Most functionality needs iphone connection

  • Dashboard Example 5

    Pros

    • Large tachometer, could be useful for manual cars
    • Great color differentiation
    • Very simplistic
    • Most used functionality is highlighted with yellow
    • Extra warning/maintenance information available

    Cons

    • Small speed gauge
    • Low lable usage for extra features
    • Low customization
    • Main be confusing for some groups at first

B Grade Section

  • Common and Rare Features

    • Common - Viewing the current RPM on the tachometer
    • Common - Viewing the current speed on the speedometer
    • Common - Viewing current miles on the vehicle
    • Common - Cruise control
    • Rare - Viewing current miles per gallon
    • Rare - Viewing map or navigation information
    • Rare - Viewing phone information

  • User Controls of Car Dashboards

    • Steering wheel, gear selecter, turn signals, gas pedal, break pedal, menu selecter, phone controls, and radio controls.

  • Common Mistakes with Current Design

    • Not differentiating between similar elements - tachometer/speedometer
    • Text sizing on menus/smaller gauges
    • Use of color, either too distracting or too Monochromatic
    • Not utilizing power of smartphones
